About S&C Babysitter

S&C Babysitter find the solution for you by providing the trust, qualified & educated babysitter service at your own house. S&C Babysitter helps working parents look for reliable babysitters by recommending them to trusted and experienced babysitters. There is a general lack of trust and respect across the babysitting sector.
Cambodian working parents find it difficult to find a reliable babysitter as this is an unregulated sector and horror stories abound on social media.At the same time, babysitters feel undervalued by some working parents and feel a lack of respect and care from employers.
S&C Babysitter is an online platform that helps connect respectful parents to trusted and experienced babysitters bridging the sector’s trust gap.





S&C's Values

MISSION: Connect trusted and qualified babysitters with parents and Incorporate Early Childhood development knowledge

VISION: We aim to be the first childcare service to provide trained and qualified babysitters with Early Childhood Education.
